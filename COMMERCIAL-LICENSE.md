## What counts as commercial use

Any activity that is primarily intended for or directed toward
commercial advantage or monetary compensation. Examples include,
but are not limited to:

- **Selling or distributing** the application (or a modified
  version) as a paid product.
- **Bundling** the application into a product or service that is
  sold or monetized.
- **Offering** the application's functionality as part of a paid
  SaaS, hosted service, or subscription.
- **Internal business deployment** where the software supports
  revenue-generating operations (e.g., used by employees of a
  for-profit company as part of their work).
- **Consulting or contract work** where you deliver the software
  (or a derivative) to a paying client.

## What is allowed without a commercial license

- Personal use, hobby projects, and private experimentation.
- Academic and educational use (teaching, coursework, research).
- Use by nonprofit organizations, charities, and public
  institutions.
- Free community forks that remain noncommercial and keep the
  same PolyForm Noncommercial license.
- Contributions (pull requests, bug reports, documentation) back
  to this project.

## How to request a commercial license

Open an issue on the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/grimdyson/Scoremoro/issues)

Please include:

1. Your name or company name.
2. A brief description of how you intend to use Scoremoro.
3. The expected scale of deployment (number of users, seats, or
   distributions).
4. Any relevant timeline or launch date.

## Questions

If you are unsure whether your use qualifies as commercial,
reach out at the same address. When in doubt, ask first.
